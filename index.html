Maxwell's Equations Visualized

Electric charge makes space point outward.

A first interactive glimpse at Gauss's Law: a charge inside a transparent surface creates electric flux through that surface.

Differential form

∇ · E = ρ / ε₀

Positive charge acts like a source of electric field. Negative charge acts like a sink. The more charge enclosed, the more electric field flows through the surrounding surface.

What you are seeing

The glass sphere is not a physical object. It is an imaginary measuring surface. Gauss's Law says the net electric flux through that closed surface depends only on the charge inside it.
